Introduction

I recently graduated with my BS in Mechanical Engineering at Cornell University and will graduate with my Masters of Aerospace Engineering in December. During my time at college, I was an engineer on Combat Robotics at Cornell and an integration and test engineer for the Alpha CubeSat mission set to launch this fall. Currently, I am interning at NASA Ames Research Center in the Rotorcraft Aeromechanics division, designing rotor blade experiments and working on rotorcraft flight dynamics and controls.

As a driven, resilient, and adaptable student with a strong desire to learn, I am consistently striving to pair my academics with practical applications of engineering skills. New opportunities to expand my engineering knowledge and skills inspire me, so please feel free to contact me with the links below.
